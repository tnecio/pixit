What is PiXiT?

PiXiT is an online game inspired by a board game called Dixit. In PiXiT instead of using painted cards with dream-like imagery, users play with artistic photographs (kindly provided by Unsplash). The authors of PiXiT are not affiliated with the authors of Dixit: Jean-Louis Roubira, Marie Cardouat, nor with its publisher ‚Äì Libellud.

  • üó≥Ô∏è Voting. Once all the players have sent their card to the table, voting begins. Each of the players, except the narrator, try to guess which of the cards on the table was selected by the narrator. You cannot vote on your own card. Voting ends when all the players have cast their vote.
  • üíØ Points. After voting the points are distributed as follows:
    • If everyone guessed the narrator's card: 2 points for everyone except the narrator. (Therefore, the narrator should not make their hint too obvious).
    • If no one guessed the narrator's card: 2 points for everyone except the narrator. (Therefore, the narrator should not make their hint too difficult).
    • Otherwise, everyone whose card got voted on gets 1 point per each vote;
    • everyone who guessed the narrator's card gets additional 3 points;
    • and the narrator gets 3 points.
    Game end when at least one player gathers 30 points. After that players can immediately start the game again.
