What is PiXiT?

PiXiT is an online game inspired by a board game called DixitTM. In PiXiT instead of using painted cards with dream-like imagery, users play with artistic photographs (kindly provided by Unsplash). Please note that the authors of PiXiT are in no way affiliated with Libellud, the owner of the Dixit trademark.

Rules

  • Starting the game. Once you have created the game, a unique link will be generated. Share it with all the people you'd like to invite to the game. You need at least 3 people to play, although the more the better. Once all the players have gathered and pressed "Start" the first round of the game will begin.
  • Game screen. Here be image. The top left corner display status of all the players in the game. Your entry is highlighted. Top right corner displays instructions that will guide you throughout the game, and also acts like a game table. Cards and phrases that will appear there are visible to all the players. On the bottom you see your cards. Only you can see them.

Each of the players must now select one of the cards to try fool others into thinking that their card is the one that the narrator associated with the phrase.   • Voting. Once all the players have sent their card to the table, voting begins. Each of the players, except the narrator, try to guess which of the cards on the table was selected by the narrator. You cannot vote on your own card. Voting ends when all the players have cast their vote.
  • Points. After voting, the points are distributed as follows:
    • If everyone guessed the narrator's card: 2 points for everyone except the narrator. (Therefore, the narrator should not make their hint too obvious).
    • Otherwise, everyone whose card got voted on gets 1 point per each vote;
    • everyone who guessed the narrator's card gets an additional 1 point;
    • and the narrator gets 3 points if anyone voted on their card.
