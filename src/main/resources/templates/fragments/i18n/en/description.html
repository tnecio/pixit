What is PiXiT?

PiXiT is an online game inspired by a board game called Dixit. In PiXiT instead of using painted cards with dream-like imagery, users play with artistic photographs (kindly provided by Unsplash). Please note that the authors of PiXiT are in no way affiliated with the authors of Dixit: Jean-Louis Roubira, Marie Cardouat, nor with their publisher ‚Äì Libellud.

  • üíØ Points. After voting, the points are distributed as follows:
    • If everyone guessed the narrator's card: 2 points for everyone except the narrator. (Therefore, the narrator should not make their hint too obvious).
    • Otherwise, everyone whose card got voted on gets 1 point per each vote;
    • everyone who guessed the narrator's card gets an additional 1 point;
    • and the narrator gets 3 points if anyone voted on their card.
